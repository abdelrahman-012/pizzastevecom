About Pizza Palace

Welcome to Pizza steve, where passion meets flavor! Since 1995, we've been serving the most delicious and authentic pizzas in town. Our secret lies in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Located in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere for families, friends, and pizza lovers. Whether you're dining in or ordering for delivery, we guarantee a memorable experience with every bite.

Meet our talented team of chefs who pour their heart into every pizza they make. From classic Margherita to gourmet specialties, we have something for everyone. Come visit us and taste the difference!
